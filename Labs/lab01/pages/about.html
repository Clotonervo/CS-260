About Page

Welcome to my photographer portfolio! Here I have included a little "About Me" section so you can get a better idea as to what I do and why I do it!


Who am I?

My name is Mike, and I am a turtle enthusist! I grew up in Portland, Oregon, where my favorite hobbies were photography, smelling essencial oils, and talking about how weird Portland is! I love to wear scarves and shirts that say "Keep Portland Weird". I also love coffee shops, but I only smell the coffee and hang out there.

What do I do?

As I said before, I am a turtle enthusist! I love learning about turtles and I truly believe they are the most fascinating creatures that live on here. If I could, I would turn into a turtle forever! How nice would it be to always have a warm home on your back that you could go to whenever you are cold or stressed or have a CS 260 lab due? It would be awesome! So to share my fascination, I photograph turtles professionally!

This is me looking for more turtles! Did you know that tutles are superior to tortoises because they can swim and go on land? Don't let anyone tell you otherwise!

If you ever have any photo opportunities for me, please let me know! I love traveling to different places to try and find more turtles to photograph! Head over to my Contact page for more information!
