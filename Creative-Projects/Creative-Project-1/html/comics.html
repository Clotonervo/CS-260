My Favorite Comics

Get to know a little bit more about me though my favorite comics


Something that I have always enjoyed are comics. I find enjoyment in a wide variety of themes, (and quite frankly I read the comics page of the newspaper every Sunday. Here is a list of some of my favorite comics, and you can click the button to show an example of them!

Xkcd

Now this is kinda nerdy becuase these comics make a lot of jokes that have to deal with programmers, physists, and other science things that only geeks understand. It was created by an ex-rocket scientist who used to work for NASA, and he also does a lot of rediculous calculations that people have asked him to do in regards to super outrageous situations. One of my favorites is the question "How many AK-47s do you need to fire at the ground in order to actually treat the guns as a jetpack". The answer ended with around 47 guns.


Calvin and Hobbs

A classic comic, known for its existential themes, blanketed by stupid childlike activities. Absolutely one of the best comics out there


The Far Side

Normally these come in calendar comics, where you view one a day. Man these are brilliantly stupid.


FoxTrot

These normally come in the Sunday edition of newspapers. They are also extremely nerdy comics, but they are always the best on the page.
