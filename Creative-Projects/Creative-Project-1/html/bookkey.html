BookKey is a mobile app for iOS users that I am currently developing. I am designing it to better help you keep track of your books, find new book recommendations based on what you have read, and also price compare multiple sources so that you can get the best bang for your buck!

Every book ever printed is associated with a unique identifier known as an ISBN. These are used to differenciate the edition, title, and price. BookKey uses the ISBN to quickly search and provide information from a number of different websites so you can simply can the book ISBN and see the information pop up!

Right now, BookKey is still in development, and hopefully soon there will be screenshots that will help those understand a little bit more about how incredible this could be for students, teachers, avid readers and book clubs!
